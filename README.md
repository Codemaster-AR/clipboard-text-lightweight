**Clipboard Text Lightweight**
================================

**Overview**
------------

A lightweight clipboard manager, designed for simplicity and ease of use. It's free, offline, and safe to use. Coming soon to Windows, currently available for Mac.

**Features**
------------

- **Free and Offline**: Use Clipboard Text Lightweight without any restrictions or online dependencies.
- **Easy to Use**: Simple and intuitive design makes it a breeze to manage your clipboard.
- **Safe**: Trust in the security of our clipboard manager.

**Platforms**

- **Mac**: Download for Mac on our GitHub page.
- **Windows**: Coming soon.
